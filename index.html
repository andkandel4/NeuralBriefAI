QUESTIONS.

Do I need to know how to code?
No. n8n is a visual drag-and-drop tool. You import the file, fill in your details, and activate. The setup guide walks through every step in plain English.
How much does it cost to run?
Almost nothing. The Groq API (which writes your emails) is free. n8n has a free self-hosted version. You pay $49 once and the system runs for free indefinitely.
Can I customize the emails for my brand?
Yes. You add your brand name, colors, shop link, and tone. The AI adapts to your brand voice. The setup guide shows you exactly where to make changes.
What if I only want one type of email?
You can turn off any of the three types and run just the ones you want. The workflow is fully customizable once it's imported.
How long does setup take?
Most people are live in under 30 minutes. Importing the workflow takes seconds. The main time is getting your Groq API key and connecting Gmail — both take about 5 minutes each.
